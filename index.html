About Us

Building Nepal's Next Generation of Global Leaders

Founded in 2001, Xavier International College has grown from a single-campus institution into one of Nepal's most respected centers of higher education. With over 25 years of academic excellence, we combine rigorous scholarship with real-world practice to prepare students for an interconnected, fast-changing world.

Our Vision To be Nepal's most trusted institution for transformative, globally-relevant education.
Our Mission Delivering rigorous, accessible education that builds leadership, character, and skill.
Our Values Integrity, Innovation, Inclusion, and Impact guide everything we do.
Why Xavier Industry-linked curriculum, global exchange, and a 98% placement record.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the minimum eligibility requirements for admission?

Most undergraduate programs require completion of +2 (or equivalent) with a minimum GPA of 2.0, along with a passing score on the Xavier Entrance Examination.

How do I apply for a scholarship?

Scholarship applications open alongside general admissions. You can indicate scholarship interest directly on the online application form — merit awards are determined by entrance exam and academic record.

Is on-campus hostel accommodation available?

Yes. Separate hostels for men and women are available on a first-come, first-served basis, with meal plans and 24/7 security included.

Can I transfer credits from another institution?

Credit transfers are evaluated case-by-case by the relevant department head. Please contact Admissions with your transcripts for an assessment.

Do you accept international students?

Absolutely. International applicants follow the same process with additional document verification and visa support provided by our International Office.

What is the fee structure and payment schedule?

Fees vary by program and are payable per semester. Download the prospectus above for a full breakdown, or contact our Admissions office for a personalized estimate.
